What is Maple

Maple is a calculator. More specifically, Maple is a calculator on really strong steroids. The kind that make scrawny wimpy kids into huge football players. Or... your computer into the biggest and baddest calculator you've ever seen. Type one quick command and Maple will solve an integral. Type another command and it will solve a system of equations, or matrix multiplication. Another command solves any differential equation you can come up with. Maple makes your TI-84 look like an abacus.
