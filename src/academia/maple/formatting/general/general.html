Formatting: General

Formatting is key if you plan on receiving any credit for your work. People, be they your teacher, professor, grader, peer, or what have you, must be able to read your work. This page will show you how to format your work in a way that is easy and quick to read. I'll also give you tips in formatting to avoid errors.

Collapsible Headings
My favorite part of Maple is the inclusion of collapsible headings. To create a new heading, use the keyboard shortcut CTRL + PERIOD. If you've already started your problem and have many lines of code, highlight them all and press CTRL + PERIOD. Download an example of how I use collapsible headings here.
Info! To undo a heading, use CTRL + COMMA.
