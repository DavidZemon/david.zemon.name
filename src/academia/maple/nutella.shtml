Nutella

By now, you're probably getting pretty tired of Maple errors. They happen. A lot. Almost always it's a user error, but we never admit it. They can be aggravating and make you want to jump off a bridge, or worse yet, throw your computer out the window of your seven story dorm room.

But fear not! For Nutella is here to rescue you. Whenever you're feeling down, whenever you feel Maple has had the best of you, just follow these simple instructs:

How To: Make Nutella Toast
Step 1) Buy (or bake) whole-grain bread
Step 2) Buy Nutella (oh yea... see where this is going?)
Step 3) Take a slice of bread, insert into toaster oven
Step 4) Remove 30 seconds before finished
Step 5) Glop copious of Nutella on one side of the bread
Step 6) Return to toaster oven for 30 seconds
Step 7) Remove and enjoy!

*Caution, contents will be hot. And won't last long. Make lots.
