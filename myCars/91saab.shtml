1991 Saab 900S

This was my first car. My uncle gave it to me in May of 2007. It was his old car, and it was my 16th birthday present. This car was my true love until one terrible morning, I was rear ended on the way to school. Because these cars are monocoque, meaning the body panels are also the frame, the car was totaled.

With the insurance money, I purchased Volkswagen's best-kept secret: a Corrado.
