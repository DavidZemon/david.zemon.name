1993 Saab 900S

Purchased in late fall of 2009 for $800, my '93 was one of my absolute favorites. With only 132,000 miles, it was an excellent car and hopefully still is for its current owner. The excellent interior was matched perfectly with a dark, metallic green paint that enjoyed frequent hand waxes. She ran very strong with the 2.1L naturally aspirated engine and the low-end torque never ceased to amaze me. I write this now, two years after selling it, and have honestly forgotten most of what made her unique. Mostly what I remember is 100% reliability. In two years of driving, the only necessary repair was replacement of the passenger side inner-driver. The sale was made in early fall/late summer of 2011 to a friend of STL Swede Speed for $1,000.
