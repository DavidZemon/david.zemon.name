1988 Saab 900S

This was my third car. Very much like my other Saabs, but it doesn't have leather seats (that's a plus in my opinion), only the driver's side window is one-touch down, the sun-roof is not electric, it produces about 15 less hp (B202 engine instead of B212), and that's about it. Most everything I know about cars I learned on this one. I did all sorts of repairs to this car including: head gasket, timing chain, transmission, and various other bits.
