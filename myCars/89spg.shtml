1989 Saab 900 SPG

Picture coming soon...

$2,500, 267,000 miles, a broken power steering rack, bald tires, an interior anyone ought to be ashamed of... SPG. Saab created a tuned version of the 900 known as the "SPG", or "Special Performance Group" that put out 175 HP and 195 lb-ft of torque. Combine that with stiffer springs and other beautiful details and the result is a highly sought after collector's item. My love affair continues with this gem. Hindsight 20/20, it wasn't the deal-of-a-lifetime I thought at first - but don't take that to mean I regret this perfect rolling restoration project (much unlike the '91 Turbo). As I write this, she sits in my garage with the interior fully stripped, ready for the beige interior from my '91 to be dropped in. I will be using the '91 over the next few months to create the cleanest car possible with parts freely available to me. Expect great things to come from this partnership!
