1991 Saab 900 Turbo

Purchased as an upgrade from my '93, it has been a constant love/hate relationship with her. The purchase story has its own blog entry on my [rarely used] blog. In short, I acquired her for $800 from Flint, Michigan and the fuel pump failed 60 miles out at 11pm on July 4, 2011. Since then, no major repairs have been necessary but I quickly discovered unacceptable frame rust at the passenger-side lower-front control arm mount. Combining that boost problems and general "so-so" condition, a replacement in the summer of 2013 should come as no surprise to anyone. 130,000 miles were on the clock at purchase and she now sits at aproximately 150,000. Many good parts were installed during my time with her, and I plan on taking every bit possible to be used on my next endeavour.
